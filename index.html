WillGroww : Hustle Grow & Rate

WillGroww is a productivity app designed to help individuals achieve their personal and professional goals. With the realization that one's work life takes up a significant portion of their time, the app focuses on helping users improve their productivity and achieve personal growth.

The app allows users to create checklists of their daily tasks and customize their day plan according to their preferences. It also includes a feature for daily repetitive work, making it easier for users to plan their day without having to input the same task every day.

Medibey : Revolutionizing healthcare

Medibey is a mobile app designed to make healthcare more accessible and convenient for people. With features like appointment booking, telemedicine, and healthcare provider search, users can easily find and connect with doctors, clinics, and hospitals in their area.

Medibey provides a one-stop solution for all healthcare needs, making it easy for users to manage their health and wellness. Whether it's booking an appointment, consulting with a doctor remotely, or accessing health information, the app empowers users to take control of their health and make informed decisions.

Mini Militia Clone

I developed a multiplayer battle royal game, inspired by the popular game Minimitia, using P5 Js library. The game accommodates 6 players in a single gameplay of 5 minutes. Players are required to make the most kills to win the game.

The game is designed to provide an immersive and engaging experience to the players, allowing them to strategize and compete with their friends. With its fast-paced action and thrilling gameplay, the game promises to be an exciting addition to any gaming enthusiast's collection.
